Rental Partner FAQ

Q. How do I get notifications about work orders and/or change how/when I am notified?

A. Log into the owners' web here. Once you have logged in, you will be on the "My Account" tab. Scroll to the bottom of the page to Work Order Mail Settings.  This is where you have the option to choose your notification settings, you may select to be notified for work orders that are initiated, assigned, closed, when a technician enters your property, or any combination that you prefer.

The Beach Club Receives Two National Awards

For the second consecutive year, The Beach Club was recognized as a Top 10 Family Vacation Resort by TripAdvisor's family vacation travel site, Family Vacation Critic.  Family Vacation Critic began to issue this annual award in 2014, and The Beach Club was among the first ten family resorts to receive this designation. We are honored to receive this award for the second year in a row, and excited to have received this award each year it has been presented.  To receive this award, The Beach Club had to be personally visited by a member of the Family Vacation Critic editorial team, recommended by 75% or more families that have reviewed a stay at The Beach Club, and maintain a four or five star rating on Family Vacation Critic.

Oyster.com, a TripAdvisor owned travel website, recently named The Cottages at The Beach Club as one of The 7 Most Adorable Cottages in the United States. Oyster.com states, "Tucked away from rowdy college kids, this family-friendly gated community sits on 86 acres near a large, private, white sandy beach", "The Beach Club is very clearly family-oriented, and its extensive property features and activities make it feel more like a resort than a condo complex."  You can read the full article here.
